# TeqMD Privacy Policy — Processor & Feature Addendum

Last updated: May 31, 2026

---

## 5.1.1 Named subprocessors and platform features

In addition to the processor categories in Section 5.1, TEQMD uses the following subprocessors and platform features to deliver the Services. Each processes personal data only as needed for that purpose and under contractual safeguards comparable to Philippine data protection standards.

### Hosting and database

| Subprocessor | Role | Typical data | Location |
|--------------|------|--------------|----------|
| **Railway Corp.** | Application hosting | HTTP logs, request metadata | United States (may vary by region) |
| **MongoDB Atlas** | Primary database | Account, diagnostic, booking, payment, and support data described in Section 2 | Configured cluster region (commonly Singapore or nearest available) |

### Payments

TEQMD uses one or more of the following payment gateways to process checkout. TEQMD does **not** store full card numbers; payment credentials are entered and processed on the gateway’s own secure pages or domains.

| Gateway | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **PayMongo** | Cards and Philippine e-wallets |
| **Xendit** | Cards and e-wallets |
| **HitPay** | Cards and selected payment methods |
| **PayPal** | PayPal checkout |

The gateway used for your transaction is shown at checkout.

### Video consultations

| Subprocessor | Purpose | Data involved |
|--------------|---------|---------------|
| **Zoom Video Communications** | Scheduled video meeting links | Name, email, meeting time, meeting URL |
| **Google Calendar / Google Meet** | Meet links for scheduled sessions | Same as above |
| **Microsoft Teams / Microsoft Graph** | Teams meeting links for scheduled sessions | Same as above |

Depending on your booking, one of the above providers hosts your consultation. **Audio, video, screen sharing, and in-meeting chat** may be processed on that provider’s infrastructure, which may be located outside the Philippines.

### Session recording and AI meeting notes (opt-in only)

When you **opt in at checkout**, a visible notetaker from **Fathom Video Inc.** may join your consultation to produce transcripts, summaries, and action items. TEQMD may store booking-linked metadata (such as a recording identifier, share link, and summary text) and email you a link to meeting notes.

**Recordings remain TEQMD property.** Opt-in means you consent to recording and transcription for service delivery; it does not transfer ownership of the recording to you. You may decline opt-in at checkout.

### Transactional email

TEQMD sends service-related email through one of the following providers:

| Provider | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Resend**, **Postmark**, or **SendGrid** | Booking confirmations, payment reminders, meeting notes (when you opted in), and responses to support requests |

TEQMD does not use these providers to send promotional marketing email unless you have given separate consent.

### Artificial intelligence

TEQMD uses **OpenAI** (via API) for certain product features. Inputs may include text you type in the guided diagnostic or related planning flows. Outputs are for **technology planning purposes only** — they are not legal, medical, financial, or HR advice.

| Feature | What is sent | When |
|---------|--------------|------|
| Guided diagnostic rounds | Your prompts and prior questions and answers in the session | While you use the diagnostic |
| Situation suggestions and summaries | Text from your diagnostic session | During the diagnostic |
| Web insight (optional) | A short situation description you enter; approximate location **Philippines / Asia-Manila** may be included for search context | When you choose to request web insight |
| Response caching | Derived representations of diagnostic thread text | To improve response speed; not linked to your name unless you later register or book |

Processing by OpenAI may occur in the **United States** or other regions where OpenAI operates.

TEQMD does not use fully automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you without human review (see Section 12).

### Analytics (cookie opt-in)

If you accept **analytics cookies**, TEQMD loads **Google Analytics 4** (Google LLC). We configure it with IP anonymization and without ad personalization signals from TEQMD. Analytics cookies are **not** set until you consent through the cookie banner or **Cookie preferences** link in the site footer.

### Cookies and local storage

| Name / key | Type | Purpose |
|------------|------|---------|
| `it_visitor_id` | Required cookie | Saves anonymous diagnostic progress before you sign in |
| `it_auth_session` | Required cookie | Keeps you signed in to your account |
| `techmd-cookie-consent` | Local storage | Remembers your cookie choice (required only vs analytics) |
| Appearance preferences | Local storage | Remembers display settings such as theme |
| Payment partner cookies | Third-party | Set on payment gateway domains during checkout |

See Section 10 for cookie categories (strictly necessary, functional, analytics, and security).

### Support reports

The website and TeqMD mobile app let you submit **support or bug reports**. Depending on what you include, we may collect your message, the screen or route you were on, your email address, name, phone number, an optional **screenshot**, a device identifier, and browser or app user-agent string. We store this information to investigate and respond to your report and may email you updates about your request.

### Mobile app

The TeqMD mobile app uses the same services as the website. The app may send a **device identifier** so diagnostic progress from the app can be linked to your account when you register or sign in, similar to the visitor cookie on the website.

### Diagnostic and support forms — please read

Do not enter personal information about other people (such as employees, clients, or vendors) unless you have the authority to share it and have given them appropriate notice. By submitting information in the diagnostic, booking, or support flows, you confirm that you are entitled to provide it.

### What we do not sell

TEQMD does **not** sell, rent, or trade personal data. See Section 5.
